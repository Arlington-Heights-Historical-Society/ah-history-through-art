# History through art project
Information for artists and members of the Art Guild community.

### Background context

In order to engage with our community and tell the stories in our collection, we propose to organize an event that tells stories using artistic expression, mostly through the creation of paintings, images and videos. The event will be the culmination of an open competition where artists can submit their work to tell the stories that inspire them.  
These stories will be constrained to two themes. The first is the story of Harmony Park. The second are stories from the HANA neighborhood and houses.
